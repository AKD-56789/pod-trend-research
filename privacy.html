Privacy Policy

POD Trend Research · Last updated: July 17, 2026

1. Who we are

POD Trend Research is a personal, non-commercial application operated by a single individual (the developer). It is used privately for product trend research and is not offered as a service to any other person or business.

2. What data the application accesses

The application reads publicly available, aggregated keyword trend data through the official Pinterest API v5 (trends endpoints). It does not access Pins, boards, user profiles, messages, ads data, or any personal data of Pinterest users.

3. What data we collect from visitors

This website does not use cookies, analytics, tracking technologies, or contact forms, and does not collect any personal data from visitors.

4. How data is used and stored

Aggregated trend keywords retrieved from the API are used solely for short-term, internal product research by the developer. Data is not warehoused long-term, and is never shared, sold, licensed, or distributed to any third party.

5. Third parties

The application communicates only with the official Pinterest API. Use of the Pinterest API is subject to Pinterest's Developer Terms, which the developer complies with, including its data storage and usage requirements.

6. Your rights and contact

Because no personal data of any user or visitor is collected, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. For any question about this policy, contact: nguyenhungx7ht@gmail.com

7. Changes to this policy

Any change to this policy will be published on this page with an updated date.
